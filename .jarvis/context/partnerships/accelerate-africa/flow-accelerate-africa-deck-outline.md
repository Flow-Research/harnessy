# Flow x Accelerate Africa Deck Outline

Date: 2026-03-11
Status: Draft

## Slide 1 - Title

Flow

The apprenticeship infrastructure for Africa's AI economy.

Sub-line:
Learn through work. Earn from day one. Progress into higher-value human+AI roles.

## Slide 2 - The Problem

- Africa has massive human potential, but weak pathways into high-quality digital work.
- Businesses need reliable execution capacity, but junior talent is expensive to onboard and hard to trust.
- Traditional training is disconnected from income and real demand.

Close with:
Potential is abundant. Trusted pathways into productivity are not.

## Slide 3 - Why Now

- AI lowers the cost of supervision, support, task decomposition, and QA.
- Digital work demand is expanding globally.
- Mobile and internet access continue to improve across Africa.
- The labor market pressure is urgent and structural.

Message:
This is the first time it is economically viable to train through real work at scale.

## Slide 4 - The Solution

Flow is an AI-native work and learning platform where people enter through real tasks, not abstract courses.

Simple flow:
- workers onboard
- AI assistant builds profile and supports execution
- businesses submit scoped work
- Flow matches, supports, reviews, and pays
- workers progress into higher-value work over time

## Slide 5 - How It Works

Three layers:

1. Onboarding and AI assistant
2. Task marketplace and execution workflow
3. Progression engine and trust graph

Optional visual:
Onboard -> Match -> Execute -> Review -> Earn -> Level Up

## Slide 6 - Why It Wins

- Workers earn while they learn
- Businesses get reliable, trainable execution capacity
- AI increases worker productivity instead of replacing participation
- Flow compounds proprietary data on trust, quality, and progression

Message:
We are not just matching labor. We are building the system that turns potential into productive capacity.

## Slide 7 - Business Model

- Businesses pay for completed work and managed execution
- Flow earns through margin, workflow tooling, QA, and coordination
- Over time: premium workflow products, enterprise tools, deeper AI layers

Message:
Apprenticeship is not a side initiative. It is the supply engine of the business.

## Slide 8 - Market and Strategic Opportunity

- Future of work + AI productivity + African labor market transformation
- Starting in Africa, with a model that can become globally relevant
- Strong alignment with major market themes: talent, AI, digital work, productivity infrastructure

Keep this slide disciplined: large opportunity, but framed through the narrow first wedge.

## Slide 9 - Vision

Today:
An apprenticeship pipeline for AI-compatible work.

Tomorrow:
A broader human+AI economic system where contribution, trust, and value creation are coordinated more fairly.

Message:
We start with a sharp wedge, but we are building toward a much larger system.

## Slide 10 - Why Accelerate Africa / The Ask

We want Accelerate Africa's help with:

- sharpening the launch wedge
- refining GTM and pricing
- securing pilot customers and strategic introductions
- strengthening our fundraising story for pre-seed / seed

Close with:
Flow is building from Africa to the world: a new pathway into the AI economy for the people who have the talent, but not yet the access.
