## 50-Word Company Description

Flow is building the apprenticeship infrastructure for Africa's AI economy. We help people learn by doing real work, earn from day one, and progress into higher-value human+AI roles. Businesses get reliable, trainable execution capacity; workers get income, skill growth, and a trusted pathway into the future of work.

## 100-Word Company Description

Flow is an AI-native work and learning platform that helps African talent enter the AI economy through real tasks, embedded learning, and fast earnings. Instead of separating education from income, Flow turns work itself into an apprenticeship pipeline. Businesses get reliable execution capacity supported by AI, workflow tooling, and quality control. Workers start with structured tasks, build trust through contribution, and progress into more complex, better-paid human+AI roles over time. Our long-term vision is to build the economic infrastructure for a more inclusive AI economy, starting with a practical wedge: helping people learn, earn, and become productive through real work.

## What Problem Are You Solving?

Africa has a rapidly growing workforce, but too few trusted pathways into high-quality digital work. At the same time, businesses increasingly need flexible, AI-enabled execution capacity, but cannot efficiently onboard junior talent without sacrificing quality. Existing systems split training from income: people learn in theory, but struggle to convert that into paid work. Flow solves that gap by turning real work into structured apprenticeship. We help workers earn while they learn, and help businesses access reliable, trainable talent supported by AI and managed workflows.

## Why Now?

AI changes the economics of supervision, support, and task decomposition. It is now possible to break work into manageable units, guide workers with AI assistance, and maintain quality at lower coordination cost than before. At the same time, Africa's labor market pressure is intensifying, and digital work demand continues to grow globally. This is the right moment to build a system that turns underutilized talent into productive human+AI operators. The opportunity is no longer theoretical: the enabling technology is here, and the market need is urgent.

## Why Is This Important For Africa?

Africa's challenge is not only unemployment. It is the lack of scalable systems that convert potential into trusted income. Millions of capable people remain excluded from opportunity because they lack credentials, experience, or access to the right networks. Flow creates a practical pathway into the AI economy by combining work, learning, and earnings inside one system. That matters because it creates income today, capability over time, and a stronger labor and productivity base for African businesses and workers. We see this as economic infrastructure, not just a marketplace.

## What Makes Flow Different?

Most platforms either train people without guaranteeing income, or match people to jobs without helping them become productive. Flow combines both. We do not treat apprenticeship as a side program; it is the core operating model. We use AI to lower the cost of support, task decomposition, communication, and quality assurance, making it possible to onboard and grow talent through real work. Over time, this creates a defensible worker graph built on contribution, reliability, and progression, not just resumes and self-claimed skills.

## Who Are Your Customers?

Our immediate paying customers are businesses that need reliable, trainable execution capacity for AI-compatible operational work. Our users are workers entering the platform through a structured task pipeline. We design for both sides of the market from day one: businesses need quality and speed; workers need trust, earnings, and progression. Flow exists to make those two sides work together efficiently.

## What Is The Business Model?

Businesses pay Flow for completed work and managed execution. We capture margin through matching, workflow tooling, AI support, and quality assurance. Over time, as we build deeper data and trust systems, we can add premium software and workflow products on top of the core marketplace layer. Our long-term moat comes from the operating system we build around human+AI work, not from transaction fees alone.

## Why Are You The Right Team?

We are building Flow from direct engagement with the realities of African talent, digital work, and AI-mediated coordination. Our conviction comes from seeing the mismatch between raw capability and actual opportunity, and from believing that AI can widen participation rather than narrow it. We are not starting from a generic marketplace thesis. We are starting from a systems view: how do people learn, contribute, earn, and become more valuable over time inside a network that includes both humans and AI agents?

## What Is Your Long-Term Vision?

Our long-term vision is a sustainable economic system where both human workers and AI agents participate in value creation, and where people are rewarded not only for labor, but for the intelligence and contribution they add to the network over time. We are not leading with that full vision in the market. We are starting with the most concrete wedge: apprenticeship through real work. But we believe this can grow into one of the foundational work and productivity systems for the AI era.

## Why Accelerate Africa?

Accelerate Africa is a strong fit because Flow sits at the intersection of future of work, AI-native productivity, and African economic opportunity. We are early enough to benefit from deep operator support, but focused enough to translate that support into product, customer, and fundraising progress. We are especially interested in Accelerate Africa's strengths in storytelling, product refinement, go-to-market, and access to founder and investor networks that understand what it means to build from Africa to the world.
